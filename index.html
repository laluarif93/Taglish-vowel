Taglish Vowels

IY vs IH

IY is Fully closed I while IH is mixed of IY and EE.
Phonemes Sample-1 Sample-2 Sample-3
IY Rin
 Feet
 Ingay
IH Link
 Scholarship
 Thing

EE vs EH vs AE

EE is close-mid (close to IY) E while EH is open-mid (close to A) E.
AE is mixed of A and EH with dominant A.
Phonemes Sample-1 Sample-2 Sample-3
EE Keng
 Eddie
 Pre
EH Eggs
 Del
 Abe
AE Cat
 Band
 Catch

A vs AA vs AH

Both A and AA is fully opened. The difference between these phonemes is AA involve back tongue to slightly constrict the airflow while A doesnt
Pronunciation of A is light and clear. AH is AA with a bit AX sounds
Phonemes Sample-1 Sample-2 Sample-3
A Pla
 Mam
 Ara
AA Card
 Coggin
 Knock
AH Lang
 Kanyang
 Electoral

AX vs UH

AX and UH has similar pronunciation. UH is rounded (lips) type with a little bit UW sounds
Phonemes Sample-1 Sample-2 Sample-3
AX Angel
 Aytem
 Cinram
UH Alls
 Fulfills
 Neural

UW vs OH vs AO

UW is produced by closing mouth and rounding lips
OH is close-mid (close to UW) O while AO is open-mid (close to AA) O
Phonemes Sample-1 Sample-2 Sample-3
UW Tru
 Umo
 Luz
OH Ano
 Ito
 Ministro
AO Diyos
 Jhon
 Gov
